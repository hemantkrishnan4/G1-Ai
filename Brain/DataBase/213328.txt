The name "Iron Man" can refer to two different things:

* **Iron Man (Tony Stark):** A fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He is a billionaire industrialist, genius inventor, and playboy who dons a technologically advanced powered armor suit to fight evil. He is a founding member of the Avengers and one of the most popular superheroes in the world.
[Image of Iron Man (Tony Stark) superhero]

* **Ironman Triathlon:** A series of long-distance triathlon races organized by the World Triathlon Corporation (WTC). The races consist of a 2.4-mile (3.9 km) swim, a 112-mile (180.2 km) bicycle ride and a marathon 26.22-mile (42.2 km) run completed in that order, a total of 140.6 miles (226.3 km). The Ironman is considered one of the most challenging one-day sporting events in the world.
